About Me

Who Am I?

I'm a teacher and game designer who is passionate about creating fun projects to share with my students. I'm originally from Florida, but I've been working in South Korea since 2006.




My Hobbies



Programming

I started studying programming a couple of years ago. I started learning the basics through Python. More recently, I published an Android game written in C# and I wrote the mini-game found on this site in Javascript.

I really enjoy learning and applying my studies to projects that I can share with people.




Games

I love playing games! Games can be great tools for teaching and strengthening friendships. I've been trying to integrate more games into my lesson plans over the last few years.




Aside from gaming and programming, I enjoy being active by playing basketball or going for a run. I love eating new dishes, travelling, and spending time with my family and friends.
