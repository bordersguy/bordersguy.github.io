Classroom Creations

Educational Projects

Since 2006, I've been creating all kinds of teaching resources for students and teachers. I aim to educate through fun while ensuring that my students are achieving their educational objectives.




Academic Programs

I've had the unique experience of working for 10years in South Korea in a variety of teaching environments. In each school I've worked in I've been responsible for both creating and supervising the creation of different academic programs.


English Centers

English centers are special schools where students are immersed into classrooms which simulate real-life places, such as a restaurant, supermarket, or a hospital.

I was fortunate to join the opening teams of 3 different English centers. At one center, I was responsible for almost the entirety of the program content.


Public Schools

I've taught students from kindergarten to middle school. In addition to daily teaching, I created content for summer and winter English camps.




Curriculum Design
