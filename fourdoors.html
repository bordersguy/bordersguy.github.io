Four Doors - A digital board game



Four Doors

In this game your students will pick a character and venture around the board collecting treasure, fighting monsters....and review their English lesson through various puzzles. Teachers can input any expressions or vocabulary words directly into the game.


Adding Your Review Questions


Set Up Menu

You can input questions and vocabulary words in the setting panel on the main menu.


Set Up Menu

The program will automatically create questions from your input.



Game Details


Choices

By answering questions, the players can earn gold for their characters and then adventure to gain more strength or find more treasure.



Character Image

Each character starts with varied amount of gold, strength, and health. I use these as points. The character with the highest amount of gold, strength, and health is the winner.
