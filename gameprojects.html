Game Development

Game Projects

I started designing computer games in 2013. I started with Multimedia Fusion 2 to design some games to use in my classrooms. In 2015 I started using Unity (C#) to make games. I published my first game in 2016 in the GooglePlay store. I'm working on building more games for my students in 2017 with Phaser (JavaScript). The game found in the header of the page is an example of what I'm aiming to do.

Cursed Shadows (concept)


This is a project I worked on to organize a concept I had for a game. This is a "napkin sketch" of what I'm hoping to build in the future. The concept is that you are an adventurer who has been struck with curse that both hinders and provides some advantages. The curse has a will of its own and will grow in strength as the game progresses. As the curses grows stronger so do you. However, if the curse becomes too strong then your body will be taken over completely and you will lose the game.

Clicking on the screenshot will take you to a demo of the concept. You may experience some glitches.
