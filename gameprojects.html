Cursed Shadows


The concept of the game, which is in early development, is that you are an adventurer who has been struck with curse that both hinders and provides some advantages. The curse has a will of its own and will grow in strength as the game progresses. As the curses grows stronger so do you. However, if the curse becomes too strong then your body will be taken over completely and you will lose the game.

The basic systems are being developed now. You can play around with the demo on the right. The game is not being developed for browser play, so you will see some glitches.

Circles Vs. Squares 1:
Circle's Fight

This is a simple platformer with 5 levels. It's intended to be introduced in a classroom and setup by a teacher. The teacher needs to input some vocabulary words before the students play. The vocabulary words get scrambled and then must be unscrambled to unlock different levels. You can play the game on the left.



Circles Vs. Squares 2:
Space Hangman

A mashup of Asteroids and Hang-man. Like Circle's Fight, it needs to be set up by the teacher before the students play. The vocabulary words are hidden and slowly revealed as the student shoots the letters that are floating around them. You can play the game on the right.
